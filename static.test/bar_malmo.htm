Our fourth bar for the passionate BrewDog fans in Sweden is set in the south coast city that links all others - Malmö.

Five minutes from Central Station and a short walk east of Gustav Adolfs Torg, this is the perfect site for our two-floor twenty-tap craft beer hideout!

BrewDog Malmö serves up the freshest BrewDog beer plus a series of curated guest brews – with kick-ass brews sourced often from right here in Malmö, or as far afield as the nether regions of the world.

The bar also serves a series of amazing burgers and hot dogs (& much more!) to pair with your beer of choice or to take home, so you can take the BrewDog Malmö vibe with you! 

As if all of the above wasn't enough, shuffleboard has made its way to Brewdog Malmö to join our already stellar collection of pastime activities - including our N64 with multiple games, plus foosball and plenty of board games - to keep you entertained!
